Detailed Description

QCircuit translation context.

Stores all information relevant to execution (i.e. generation of a QCircuit). Each classical and quantum bit in the AST is mapped to a unique classical or quantum index corresponding to a QCircuit qubit or bit. Measurements are always non-destructive
